Casa Luz del Bosque translates into "Light of the Forest" in english.  This is our beautiful home in the Costa Rican Jungle. Located up on the hill in the village of Maderal, 10 minutes away from Allegria, 15 minutes from La EcoVilla, 10 minutes to Casa Sula and 5 minutes to San Mateo.

The house was built in 2010 by a Canadian developer and freshly renovated in 2021. It features 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a large tiled patio with outside furniture. Laundry room with new washer and drier, walk in closet in the master bedroom, AC in the second bedroom. Sliding doors in all front-facing rooms leading to the patio.  The house is Eco-Friendly with a solar water heating system (new as of 2021).
