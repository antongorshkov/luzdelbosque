Casa Luz del Bosque translates into "Light of the Forest" in english.  This is our beautiful home in the Costa Rican Jungle. Located up on the hill in the village of Maderal, 5 minutes away from Allegria, 12 minutes from La EcoVilla, 8 minutes to EcoVilla San Mateo and 5 minutes to San Mateo.

The house was built in 2010 by a Canadian developer and freshly renovated in 2021. It features 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, a large tiled patio with outside furniture. Laundry room with new washer and drier, walk in closet in the master bedroom, AC in the second bedroom. Sliding doors in all front-facing rooms leading to the patio.  Other features:

  • Eco-Friendly with a solar water heating system (new as of 2021)
  • High-Speed Fiber Optic Internet(100Mbps uploads and downloads!)
  • Located almost at the end of a very quite street, only two neighbors below, no car traffic
  • Ocean views from west side of the house
  • Mango and Jackfruit trees on the property
  • Large protected forest area across from the house, with hiking trails
  • Approximate House Location: https://maps.app.goo.gl/2RQd17mH752Xz7e66

Rent is $1500/month + Utilities. Utilities are Electricity(amount depends on A/C usage), Water(approx $15/month) & Internet($50/month).
